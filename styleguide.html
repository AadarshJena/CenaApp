The taste of a
confident, warm AI product.

Cena is dish-first restaurant discovery. The brand pairs the precision of Linear and Stripe with the warmth of a carrot pulled from the soil — cream, orange, leaf-green, and warm ink. This page is the living spec.

01 — Color

Organic, carrot-inspired roles

Every color is nudged warm — no cold grays. Each swatch prints its measured WCAG contrast against the cream background; carrot is restricted to large/bold text where its ratio is honest.

AA normal text ≥4.5   AA large ≥3.0 (bold ≥16px / ≥24px)   low decorative only — ratios computed live in-page.

06 — Voice

Confident, concise, a little warm

Do

  • “Start with the craving, not the restaurant.”
  • “Rate the pad see ew, not just the restaurant.”
  • “Find it nearby. Reserve in a tap.”
  • Short sentences. Verbs lead. One idea per line.

Don't

  • “The yummiest reservations app ever!!!”
  • Buzzword stacks & AI-hype superlatives.
  • Invented metrics, testimonials, partner logos.
  • Cutesy mascots or exclamation spam.
