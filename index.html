Capabilities

One agent. The whole dining loop.

Most apps hand you a search box and wish you luck. Cena is an agent that does all the work end to end, getting sharper the more you lean on it.

01 · Finds your food

Describe the craving. It finds the plate.

Tell Cena what you're in the mood for — a flavor, texture, or budget — in plain language. The agent reads the craving and surfaces the specific dishes near you, ranked by how well each plate matches, with the reason it picked them. No more sifting through online menus to find the dishes you want.

Found the plate? The agent takes it from there. It reserves directly through Cena where we support it, or through the reservation system a restaurant already uses. You just get the table. No hassle on the side.
